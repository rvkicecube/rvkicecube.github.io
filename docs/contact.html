Frequently Asked Questions

About Events

Q: How often do you host events?
A: We’re planning to host regular events throughout the year. The exact frequency will depend on community interest and venue availability.

Q: Do I need to own Magic cards to participate?
A: No! All cards are provided as part of the cube draft experience. Just bring yourself and basic lands if you have them.

Q: What skill level do I need to participate?
A: All skill levels are welcome! Whether you’re new to Magic or a seasoned veteran, our events are designed to be fun and educational for everyone.

Q: How much do events cost?
A: Entry fees vary by event but are kept affordable to encourage participation. Specific pricing will be announced with each event.

About Cube Draft

Q: What is cube draft?
A: Cube draft is a Magic format where players draft from a curated collection of cards rather than random booster packs. It’s considered one of the most skill-intensive and enjoyable ways to play Magic.

Q: How long do events typically last?
A: A typical event lasts 4-6 hours, including drafting, deck building, and tournament rounds.

Q: Can I see the cube list?
A: We’ll publish our cube list on this website once it’s finalized. Transparency is important to us!

Getting Involved

Q: Can I help organize events?
A: Absolutely! We’re always looking for volunteers to help with various aspects of event organization. Contact us if you’re interested.

Q: Do you need judges?
A: Yes! If you’re a certified judge or interested in judging, we’d love to hear from you.

Q: Can I contribute to cube design?
A: We welcome feedback and suggestions from the community. Experienced cube designers are especially encouraged to reach out.

Venue & Location

Event Locations

We’re currently finalizing venue arrangements for our events. We’re looking for locations in Reykjavík that offer:

  • Adequate Space: Room for multiple draft pods
  • Good Lighting: Important for reading cards
  • Comfortable Seating: For long tournament days
  • Accessibility: Easy to reach by public transport
  • Parking: Available for those driving

Venue Suggestions

Know a great location for Magic events? We’d love to hear your suggestions! We’re particularly interested in:

  • Game stores with event space
  • Community centers with available rooms
  • Libraries with meeting spaces
  • Cafes or restaurants with separate areas
